Authentication methods

Configure the authentication methods that members of your workspace can use to log in to Height.

Google authentication

Members can log in with a Google account.

Apple authentication

Members can log in with an Apple account.

Email & password

Members can set an email and password to log in with.

or

Single Sign-On (SSO) Business plan

Okta, Google Workspace, Azure, OneLogin, and custom SAML and OpenID.

This setting applies to workspace members only. Invited guests can still sign up and log in with an email and password.
